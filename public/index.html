Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about CME Agent.

How does the AI know each state's CME requirements?

+
Our AI maintains a continuously updated database of CME requirements for all 50 states, DC, and US territories. It tracks total hours, category-specific mandates (like ethics, opioid prescribing, pain management), renewal cycles, and special requirements. When regulations change, our AI updates within 48 hours—so you're always working with current information.

Can I really satisfy multiple states with a single course?

+
Yes—that's exactly what our AI Course Optimizer does. It analyzes the overlapping requirements across all your state licenses and recommends courses that count toward multiple states simultaneously. Physicians regularly tell us this alone saves them 20+ hours per year and hundreds of dollars in course fees.

Is my data secure and HIPAA compliant?

+
Absolutely. CME Agent uses bank-level AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. We're HIPAA compliant and never share your data with third parties. Your compliance data is yours. We also offer SOC 2 Type II compliance for enterprise customers.

What happens when I upload a CME certificate?

+
Snap a photo or upload a PDF. Our AI scans the certificate using OCR, extracts the provider, accreditation body, credit hours, credit type, and completion date. It then automatically maps those credits to the correct categories for each of your state licenses and updates your compliance dashboard in seconds.

How does the admin dashboard work for staffing agencies?

+
The admin dashboard gives your credentialing team a bird's-eye view of every physician's compliance status across every state. You can add physicians to your roster, track their credits in real time, get AI-powered risk alerts when someone falls behind, and generate audit-ready reports with a single click. Role-based access lets you assign different permissions to coordinators, compliance officers, and directors.

How far in advance does the AI alert me about deadlines?

+
Our AI begins generating proactive alerts and personalized action plans 6 months before each renewal deadline. As deadlines approach, alert frequency increases—from monthly overviews to weekly plans to urgent daily reminders. You'll never be blindsided by a renewal date again.

Do you support DEA training and other specialty requirements?

+
Yes. Beyond standard CME, our AI tracks DEA-mandated opioid prescriber training (8-hour and ongoing), state-specific requirements like cultural competency, implicit bias, infectious disease, and any specialty board maintenance of certification (MOC) requirements. Everything is tracked in one unified view.

Is there a free trial?

+
Our Free plan is free forever for any physician with a single state license. For the Pro plan, we offer a 14-day free trial with full access to the AI Course Optimizer, unlimited state tracking, and all premium features. No credit card required to start. Enterprise plans include a custom pilot for your team.
